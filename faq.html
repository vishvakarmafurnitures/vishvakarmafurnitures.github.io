Do you need help

Sometimes making a right choice for furniture can be difficult. Our experts answers the questions that are generally asked by our customers, explaining pros and cons of custom furniture Vs online furniture. For any further questions you may contact us on info@vishvakarmafurnitures.com

Should I buy ready-made furniture or get it custom made?

It depends on what you are buying

The best thing about online furniture buying is you get delivery upto 10 days depending on your location and you have option to pay in installments. However, you must understand following factors first.

Online furniture shopping can be very confusing at times. Good that it has thousands of options but it brings more confusion. They have phone support for assistance. However, meeting the expert is important who can give you right advice. Most of the images are photo shopped and can be misleading sometimes. A normal customer won't have sufficient knowledge about space and measurements. Hence, you may order something which may not be the right fit. Assembly and returns can be a hassle. It is observed that online furniture are very expensive due to their business model.

You may order the small items which are easy to ship and are not available in local area.

How do we decide whether we buy wood, aluminium, iron etc. furniture?

It depends on location

Selection of material depends on geographical location and whether condition.

If you live in urban area you have multiple choices whereas, in rural and sub-urban areas you need something durable and easy to repair items. Choosing material also has pro and cons like rusting, termite, usage etc. Talk to our expert to find out more.

How is the cost defined in custom furniture?

There are multiple options

Furniture makers decide the cost mostly on labor that goes in making furniture. You can contract out the full work by rate/sqft or rate/running ft or with material or without material. And most importantly, how fast you need the work done.

Most people think that buying a piece in intervals bring less weight on pocket. However, that is not true unless you are just replacing an item. These days interior design is very economical and one must contract out full work and you will end up saving money. Contact a good interior designer and find out or call us and understand how easy and economical it is.
